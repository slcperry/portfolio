Welcome. I am a journalism and marketing student with a passion for storytelling.

'A'ohe pau ka 'ike i ka hālau ho'okahi.

All knowledge is not taught at one school.

I was educated by and with Native Hawaiians. Our lāhui (people) value taking multiple perspectives and humbling oneself to listen and learn from others. This drives my passion for storytelling and amplifying unheard voices. Brands have a unique power to foster this open-minded sense of community. I am excited to work on teams who share a dedication to this message.
